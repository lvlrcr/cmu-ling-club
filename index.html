What we do

Our goal is to foster interest in languages and linguistics
and encourage students of all majors
to learn more about the field and applications of linguistics.
For more info, check out our meetings and events!

Meetings

General body meetings are every Friday from 5pm to 7pm in Baker Hall 235A!
All are welcome!
Our traditions include:
  • theming around a subfield of linguistics,
  • the Gramle, which is like Wordle but with spectrograms,
  • sweet treats,
  • and/or more!
